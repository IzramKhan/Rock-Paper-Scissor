# 🎮 Rock-Paper-Scissors Game

A fun and interactive **Rock-Paper-Scissors** game built in Python — simple, colorful, and user-friendly.  
Play against the computer, track your score, and enjoy smooth animations with every round!

---

## 🧩 How It Works

- You’ll play rounds against the computer.
- Choose:
  - `r` → Rock 🪨  
  - `p` → Paper 📄  
  - `s` → Scissors ✂️  
- The computer randomly picks one too.
- The winner of each round is decided by the classic rules:
  - Rock beats Scissors  
  - Paper beats Rock  
  - Scissors beats Paper  
- You can play as many rounds as you want.
- Press `q` anytime to quit and view your final score.

---

## ✨ Features

✅ Simple and intuitive interface  
✅ Animated “loading” effect between rounds  
✅ Keeps track of rounds, ties, and total scores  
✅ Replay option after quitting  
✅ Colorful and emoji-rich design for fun gameplay
